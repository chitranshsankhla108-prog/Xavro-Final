# XAVRO — Client Business Details & Brand Brief

**Business type:** Done-for-you Instagram content agency for fitness coaches.

We run the Instagram content engine for fitness coaches — Reels, carousels, captions, and content calendar — so they stay in the gym while their brand grows.

| Category        | Details                                                                                      |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| What            | Done-for-you Instagram content management                                                    |

## 2. Core Positioning

XAVRO is a **premium done-for-you content agency** for fitness coaches.

It is not:

* a freelancer
* a cheap content mill
* a generic marketing agency
* a template content shop
* a social media tips page

XAVRO sells **time back, authority, premium perception, consistency, and better sales conversations**.

The client is not buying “posts.”

The client is buying a system that makes their coaching look as strong online as it actually is.

## 3. Core Promise

**You coach. We make you impossible to ignore.**

Alternative CTA-style promise:

**You coach. We handle the content.**

A fitness coach may be elite at training people, but if their content looks inconsistent, unclear, or average, prospects judge them before they ever book a call.

## 4. Main Website Idea

The website is a premium single-page scroll experience.

It should not feel like a normal agency landing page.

The approved creative concept is:

# Editorial Reel Veil

A continuous luxury film-roll ribbon / editorial veil moves through the entire website and reveals each chapter of the XAVRO story.

The reel veil represents the client’s content presence.

At first, it feels distorted, unclear, and underperforming.

As the user scrolls, the veil moves like a cinematic film roll:

* unrolling
* bending
* curving
* rotating
* revealing text
* tightening through pain points
* refining during the solution
* settling during the final CTA

The visual story is:

**distorted content → diagnosis → pain points → refinement → monthly output system → proof → CTA**

The veil is the signature visual system of the website.

It should feel continuous across the entire page.

It should not disappear and restart in each section.

## 6. Target Audience

Online fitness coaches and personal trainers in the US and UK.

* already get client results

## 23. Build Notes For Developer / Designer

The website should not be built as a normal static landing page.

The main visual system must be the continuous **Editorial Reel Veil**.

The veil must:

* stay present through the whole website
* move like a cinematic film-roll ribbon
* reveal each section
* shift from distorted to refined
* connect pain, solution, deliverables, proof, and CTA

The background should:

* use blue-black CSS gradients
* feel like soft editorial atmosphere
* not rely on a static image
* not include hard grid lines

Text animations should:

* be smooth
* be minimal
* feel premium
* avoid glitch/cyberpunk effects

The final site should feel like one continuous film scene, not separate stacked sections.
